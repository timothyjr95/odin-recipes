Fried Rice

Description

In just 20 minutes, you'll have restaurant-quality fried rice thanks to this simple recipe. You can use chicken — or any other protein that you like — to elevate this dish to main course status. Reviewers also like to add a bag of frozen veggies to make the dish a more balanced meal.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup chopped grilled chicken (Optional)
  • ½ cup chopped green onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
